About Me


I am studying in the second year of B. Tech Smart Manufacturing at IIITDM (Indian Institute of Information Technology, Design & Manufacturing), Kancheepuram.
I am working hard on my practical skills and hope to apply all I have learnt. I strive to be conscientious and meticulous about details although I exhaust myself completely. I believe projects like this truly help me push my limits.
